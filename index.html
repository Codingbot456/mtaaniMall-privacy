2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Access

Our app requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • Product Photos: To allow you to take photos of products you wish to list or sell

  • Item Verification: To capture images of items for verification purposes

Important: We do not access your camera without your permission. You will always be prompted before camera access is requested.

2.2 Photos and Media
